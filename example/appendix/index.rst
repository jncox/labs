Appendix
--------

The purpose of the appendix is to provide long form detail to topics relevant to the workshop such as additional background on the technologies used, technical deep dives, glossary of terms, etc. Labelling sections in the appendix is useful for referencing topics and terms from the lab guides.

If a workshop contains a large amount of appendix information (and especially if multiple people are contributing simultaneously), it makes sense to split the appendix doc into multiple chunks and use simple include directives (as shown in this example) or to have a dedicated Table of Contents for the appendix.
